⚔️ How to Play

🎮 Controls

Scroll or swipe through the action cards on top of the player. The highlighted card in the center is selected. Click it to use it.

⚡ Combat & Energy

Each turn you choose to Attack, Heal, or Skip. Every attack costs energy — if you run out, you can't attack!

  • You recover +2 energy automatically after each enemy turn.
  • Use Healers to restore HP or recharge energy.
  • Watch your health bar — it turns orange below 60% and red below 30%!

📈 Progression

  • Enemies get stronger as you advance — higher HP and damage.
  • New attacks and healers unlock at levels 4 and 7.

🏆 Records

Your fastest clear time is saved for each enemy you defeat. Try to beat your record! Times are shown as mm:ss — the clock pauses when you open the pause menu.
